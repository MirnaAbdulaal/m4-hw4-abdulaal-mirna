Madonna

My favourite artist is Madonna because I find her creativity really unique, and her album 'Ray of Light' merges spirituality with electronic music really well, in a way I never saw someone else do before. She is very curious about other cultures and is also very experimental, but I think what I like most about her is that she was very bold in speaking about social issues she cared about.
